How does it work ?

Initial diagnosis by deep-learning 
The user uploads a picture of the damaged leaves of the crop, and our deep-learning model analyses the image and determines the status of the crop
In order to make sure that the webapp stays light, we’ve used ONNX to deploy our PyTorch model into the heroku app. 

Seamless communication via HubSpot ChatFlow
We wanted to ensure that the user is not left in the dark after he/she determines the status of their crop. Here’s where we used Hubspot’s ChatFlow in order to enable users to get in touch with experts right away.

Contact Experts using Hubspot Contact Form:
  In case the user wants to elaborate on the issues that he’s facing, he could fill out the contact form.
